🔫 WEAPONS

WeaponHow it worksBest forUpgrades
🔫 Machine Gun Hold click or Space to spray bullets toward your mouse. Has 30 ammo — press R to reload when empty. Fast fire rate, low damage per bullet. Kiting enemies, dealing sustained damage at range High Velocity Rounds, Overclocked, Armour Piercing, Spread Shot
🗡️ Daggers Click to slash all nearby enemies in range. Instant, no projectile. Short cooldown. Hits deal good burst damage. Close-quarters, high density groups, fast clears Sharpened Daggers, Longer Reach, Triple Strike
🛡️ Shield While equipped, incoming damage hits your shield bar first. Click to bash nearby enemies back hard and restore 30 shield. Blocks enemy projectiles on bash. Shield slowly regens over time. Tanking hits, surviving boss waves, protecting against ranged Reinforced Shield, Quick Recovery, Spike Shield, Full Block

⚙️ GAME LOOP

  • Each level has 3 waves. Clear all 3 waves to advance to the next level.
  • After each wave, choose one upgrade card from 3 random options — or open the Shop to spend gold instead.
  • Enemies drop gold when killed. Spend gold in the shop on heals, ammo, and permanent buffs.
  • Bosses spawn randomly mid-wave (more likely at higher levels). Kill the boss for a big gold bonus.
  • Beat Level 10 Wave 3 to win. A victory screen will show your final score.

💀 BOSSES

💀

The Titan

Massive armoured brute. Builds momentum and charges at you at high speed. Also fires an 8-way shockwave ring. Use the Shield bash to stagger him, or the Machine Gun to shred HP from range.

🔮

The Lich

Fires volleys of 6 homing skulls that track your position. Teleports away if you get close. Keep moving in wide arcs to dodge skulls, then close in fast with Daggers.

💣

The Bomber

Plants mines every 1.3 seconds and lobs grenades at you. Watch for the pulsing orange ring — that's a live mine. Strafe around him and avoid standing still.

👻

The Phantom

Teleports directly next to you and spawns 2 ghost clones that fade after a few seconds. Also dash-attacks repeatedly. Use Shield bash to knock clones back and create breathing room.

💡 PRO TIPS

  • Reload before you need to. Running out of MG ammo mid-wave is deadly. Reload between enemy clusters.
  • Switch weapons mid-fight. MG to whittle down a group, then Daggers to finish them off fast.
  • Shield absorbs before HP. If you're low on health, switch to Shield to let it take hits while you recover.
  • Kill Rangers first. Their shots add up fast when you're dodging melee enemies at the same time.
  • Save gold for big purchases. Full Heal (150g) and Max Shield upgrades are worth hoarding for.
  • Spread Shot + Overclocked is the strongest MG build — wide spray with fast fire rate shreds crowds.
  • Triple Strike Daggers is insane for bosses — three hits per click with no cooldown reset.
  • The Lich teleports away when you get close — throw daggers first, then chase it down.
