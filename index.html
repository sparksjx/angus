⚙️ GAME LOOP

  • Each level has 3 waves. Clear all 3 to advance.
  • After each wave pick one upgrade card or open the Shop to spend gold.
  • Enemies drop gold when killed. Use it on heals and buffs.
  • Bosses spawn randomly — more likely at higher levels.
  • Beat Level 10 Wave 3 to win!

💡 PRO TIPS

  • Reload before you run out — empty gun mid-wave is deadly.
  • Switch weapons mid-fight — MG to whittle, Daggers to finish.
  • Shield absorbs damage before HP. Switch to it when low.
  • Kill Rangers first — their shots add up fast.
  • Spread Shot + Overclocked is the best MG combo.
